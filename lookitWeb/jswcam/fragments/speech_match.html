Talking faces study

  • Age range: 3 months to 2 years
  • Duration: Five minutes
  • What happens: Your child will see eight short movies (about twenty seconds each), alternating between 'Calibration' movies which show a colorful ball spinning and 'Story' videos which show a woman's face reading two different Dr. Seuss snippets. You'll see sample frames before we start.
  • What we're studying: Where your child prefers to look during the movies--at the faces that match the sound or the faces that don't?
