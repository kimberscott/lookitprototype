Learning new verbs: overview

  • Duration: About ten minutes
  • What happens: Your child will identify some familiar verbs, then hear a conversation that introduces a new verb and match it to an action
  • What we're studying: How your child learns new verbs

Calibration videos

A colorful shape moves on the left and then on the right of the screen. We include these segments so we can tell where your child is looking during the dialogue movies.

Dialogue videos

The people in each video are having a brief conversation using a new verb.

Event videos

The people on the left and right are each acting out a different verb. A voice will ask your child to find a particular action. We may ask you to close your eyes so that your own judgments can't accidentally influence your child's looking behavior.
